# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-24

This Privacy Policy explains how the Decrypt game (the "App") handles data when used on Reddit.

## 1. Data We Process
The App processes the following types of data to provide gameplay and progression:
- Reddit user ID and username (for profiles, leaderboards, and rewards).
- Gameplay state (sessions, puzzle progress, mistakes, and powerups).
- Progression data (quests, streaks, and statistics).
- Purchase fulfillment data (order IDs and purchased items).

## 2. How We Use Data
We use data to:
- Provide the game experience, leaderboards, and rewards.
- Prevent abuse and ensure fair play.
- Fulfill in-app purchases and update inventory.
- Monitor app health and resolve errors.

## 3. External Services
The App uses a language model to generate daily puzzles. The prompt sent to the model contains configuration and content constraints, and does not include user-identifying data.

## 4. Data Retention
- Session data expires after approximately 1 hour.
- Daily leaderboard and daily quest data expire after approximately 90 days.
- Account progression and inventory are retained to preserve gameplay continuity.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising, profiling, or marketing. Data is only used to operate the App and comply with Reddit policies.

## 6. User Rights
You can stop using the App at any time. Moderators can reset stored App data for their subreddit using the provided moderation tools.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or platform requirements. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance.

## 8. Contact
For privacy questions, contact the moderators of the subreddit where the App is installed or send a Reddit message to the app account.
